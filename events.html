WEDDINGS VENUES

Extraordinary celebrations begin with extraordinary venues. A bastion of refinement, The UH channels the timeless grandeur and romantic sophistication of Quezon City. The iconic hotel pairs its elegant venues with sublime service for an altogether captivating experience.

TRIANON SUITE

Fusing modern elegance with classic architecture, the Trianon Suite is a lively, beautifully conceived space. A perennial favorite for dinner and dancing, the venue can be divided into two banquet suites for smaller celebrations.

  • Indoor Venue
  • Ceremony Capacity: 100
  • Reception Capacity: 225
  • Dinner Capacity: 150

VERSAILLES SUITE

The intimate Versailles Suite is the epitome of graceful refinement. Reflecting the character of an Upper East Side home with Art Deco stylings, the beautifully appointed banquet space is filled with radiant natural light.

  • Indoor Venue
  • Ceremony Capacity: 80
  • Reception Capacity: 70
  • Dinner Capacity: 50

FOYER

The Foyer is a glamorous common space adjoining the Trianon and Versailles Suites.

  • Indoor Venue
  • Reception Capacity: 70
